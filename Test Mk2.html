スバル - Subaru in Japanese.



Announced on 22nd of October 1992, the Impreza was released in Japan in November an offered in either fwd or awd versions and as a 4dr sedan or 5dr wagon/estate.
According to a Motor Trend article written March 1992 on page 26, the name of Subaru's new compact was, initially, to be called the Loyale, displaying an official photograph of the 4dr sedan.
In late 1995, a 2dr coupe was introduced in Japan. Initial engine choices included 1.6, 1.8 and 2.0 liter naturally aspirated engines.
